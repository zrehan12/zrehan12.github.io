Loittta Shutki

Ingredients:
  • Dry Fish (250g)
  • 2-3 Garlic cloves
  • ½ tbsp of turmeric
  • 6-7 onions
  • 1 tbsp of red chilli powder
  • 3-4 tbsps of vegetable oil
  • 10-15 green chillies
  • Salt to taste
Directions:
  1. Rinse fish throughly.
  2. Coat fish with salt then rinse again.
  3. Soak in boiling water for 10-15 mins to soften the fish.
  4. Debone the fish (optional)
  5. Heat up a pan with oil and fry some garlic
  6. Add the fish when the garlic turns golden brown
  7. Add onions, chilli powder, chillies, salt and stir
  8. Add ½ cup of water and stir at intervals to prevent the fish from drying out.
  9. Stop cooking the moment the fish starts to relase oil
