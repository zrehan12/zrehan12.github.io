Thai Tuna Salad

Ingredients:
  • 70 grams canned tuna, drained
  • 1 medium red bell pepper, diced
  • Â½ red onion, diced
  • 1 cucumber, sliced thinly and diced
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Sesame seeds, as garnish
Directions:
  1. In a large bowl, break up the tuna into smaller pieces.
  2. Mix in the red bell pepper, red onion, and cucumber.
  3. Pour the sesame oil on top and stir to combine everything.
  4. Lastly add the juice of 1 lime and stir again to combine.
  5. Salt and pepper to taste and garnish with sesame seeds before serving.
